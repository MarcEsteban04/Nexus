# Nexus

> Your Digital Life, Unified.

Nexus is a modern desktop application that brings together the tools you use every day into a single, fast, and beautiful workspace. Instead of juggling dozens of apps, browser tabs, and websites, Nexus gives you one place to manage your work, finances, shopping, gaming, AI tools, and personal productivity.

Built for developers, remote workers, gamers, and anyone who spends most of their day on a computer.

---

## ✨ Vision

Modern life is fragmented.

- GitHub for code
- Slack for communication
- Shopee for shopping
- Steam for games
- ChatGPT for AI
- Google Calendar for scheduling
- Banking apps for finances
- Notes everywhere

Nexus aims to become your personal operating system—a central dashboard that helps you stay organized without constantly switching between applications.

Everything important. One desktop app.

# 🔒 Privacy

Your data belongs to you.

Nexus is designed as an **offline-first** application.

- No required account
- Local database
- Local settings
- Local search
- Optional cloud sync (future)
